FAQ

Answers to common questions about my services

What services do you offer?

I offer a range of web development services including responsive website design, web application development, UI/UX design, and front-end/back-end development using modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, and Node.js.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-3 weeks, while more complex web applications can take 1-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline estimate during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

I include two rounds of revisions in my standard project quotes. I value your feedback and work collaboratively to ensure the final product meets your expectations. Additional revision rounds can be arranged if needed.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer website maintenance packages to keep your site secure, up-to-date, and running smoothly. Services include regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and content updates as needed.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, I can arrange a milestone-based payment schedule. I accept payments via bank transfer, Fone Pay and other digital payment methods.
